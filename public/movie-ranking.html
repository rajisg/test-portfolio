The Best Movies According to RJ

My top 3 movies of all-time

  1. V for Vendetta

    A political and mind bending movie about the dangers of consolidating power.

  2. A walk to Remember

    I don't know why people discount romantic movies, they are great especially when they are made using the script off of a good book. This thing, as all good ones, teaches a good life lesson

  3. The Fault In Our Stars

    I know it is considered cliche, but lets be honest John Green's writing was unlike any others' it sort of spoke to you (or me) and I liked this and Looking for Alaska although both were sad. I enjoy quotes from his books
